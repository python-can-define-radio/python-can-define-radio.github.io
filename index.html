Learn Python
To view our curriculum:
  1. Go to Chapter 1 of the python-course
  2. Proceed through the lessons, starting with "ex_1a_print_and_inputs.md". Note: when navigating between lessons on Github, you'll see three columns. Click on the title in the left column of the table. Clicking the middle column will show the most recent edit for that file, which is usually not what a student is trying to do.

Everything else on this website
  • Frequency Bands diagram: shows the two common naming conventions (VHF/UHF/etc and X/Ku/etc)
  • Voltage-Standing-Wave Ratio (VSWR) visualizer: interactively adjust reflected power to see the resulting waves.
  • Signal recording simulator: loosely based on the Universal Radio Hacker interface for recording a signal.
