LOBSTER: Line Of Bearing Simulation & Threat Event Response

We created this game as a tool to teach Direction Finding concepts.
Examples of concepts that you demonstrate given the current features:

  • To determine the transmitter's location, it is very helpful to gather LOBs from multiple locations.
  • The error in the azimuth of a LOB is more significant when you are further from the transmitter.
  • Power level readings in dBm are often negative. (Note: the power decay formula in the simulation hasn't been checked against reality; this is one of our future goals.)
